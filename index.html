profile / projects / notes

capi_nemoo builds personal web things.

This site is my profile, project archive, and writing space. It leans toward Linux culture, digital independence, and the kind of web that still feels authored by a real person.

I like static sites, sharp interfaces, strange corners of the internet, and tools that stay understandable. I would rather publish something clear and hand-made than ship another polished but empty shell.

The goal here is simple: show what I make, what I care about, and how I think. Projects live next to essays, experiments, and a quieter personal archive instead of being split across ten platforms.

working style
