Welcome to the Guestbook

In the old internet, every personal website had a guestbook. It was a place where visitors could leave their mark, share their thoughts, and connect with the webmaster. This is that tradition, revived.

Sign the guestbook below. Your entry will be stored locally in your browser and displayed for you to see. In the spirit of the old web, this is a simple, honest system—no databases, no servers, just localStorage and good vibes.

Guestbook Etiquette

  • Be respectful and kind
  • Share your thoughts about the site
  • Link to your own website if you have one
  • No spam, no hate, no trolling
  • Remember: this is the old internet—be human

Note: Entries are stored in your browser's localStorage. They are not shared with a server or other users. This is a demo implementation—in a real guestbook, entries would be stored in a database.
